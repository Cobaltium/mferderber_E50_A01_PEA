Teaching Resource Project (TRP)

The subject of my PEA is the Teaching Resource Project (TRP) that was worked on in class with a group of students which includes:

Each member of the team worked on different portions of the project as developers.

The purpose of TRP is to allow the academic dean and college administration at Heritage College to assign teaching resources to various courses or activities at the college. The system also includes functionality which allows for better estimation by using previous years to project what the values could be. The project took place over the 5th and 6th semester of the Computer Science program.
